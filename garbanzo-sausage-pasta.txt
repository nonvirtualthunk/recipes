1 lb ground pork
	- 1 1/2 tsp fennel seed, toasted
	- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
	- 2 garlic cloves finely minced
	- 1 tsp paprika
	- 1/4 tsp ground pepper
1 egg
1/4 cup panko

1 sprig rosemary
1 container arugala
1 shallot / 4 cloes garlic
1 can garbanzo beans, rinsed and some skins removed
1/3 cup parmesan
1/2 cup wine
~1/4 cup olive oil





Combine pork sausage ingredients along with whisked egg and panko, form into large meatballs
Fry rosemary in oil ~2 minutes, then remove.
Fry in olive oil until browned all over, then break up and cook through. (can combine with previous step)
Start pasta water
Remove sausage, add garbanzo beans and garlic/shallot, fry on medium high for several minutes
Blanch arugala for 1 minute in boiling water, then cool with cold water, squeeze water out, cut fine
Start pasta, cook to toothsome -2 minutes or so
Remove some of bean mixture to sausage bowl, add wine to remainder, cook off ~3 minutes
Add ~3/4 cup pasta water and cook down
Add pasta along with more pasta water, stir on med-low heat until cooked through
Add parmesan and stir, more pasta water as necessary. Add arugala
